Cow's Milk

A single glass of 200ml of cow's milk produces 0.6kg of emissions, 0.36kg more than Rice milk (the alternative with the most emissions). The same glass takes 1.8 square metres of land, 1.65 square meters more than it's nearest plant-based alternative, Oat. It also uses 125.6 litres of water, 51.3 litres more than Almond milk (the alternative that uses the most water).

So why not make a small change, that has a big impact?
